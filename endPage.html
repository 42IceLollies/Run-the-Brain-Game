Descriptions of the other parts needed in the presentation:

Medulla: The medulla is one of the three parts of the brainstem. It regulates your heartbeat and breathing.

Cerebral Cortex: The cerebral cortex is the outer layer of the brain consisting of the four main lobes: parietal, occipital, temporal,
and frontal. It is the newest section of the brain evolution-wise, giving humans higher reasoning and processing abilities and unique
personalities.

Limbic System: The limbic system is deeper inside the brain, older than the cerebral cortex but also younger than the Old Brain. It
consists of the amygdala, hippocampus and hypothalamus, giving you rudimentary emotions, the ability to learn and store memories and it has
control of your endocrine system to release horomones.

Corpus Callosum: The corpus callosum is the connective material between the two halves of your brain. It allows communication between
the left side that thinks rationally, processing and applying logic to your experience of the world and the less linear, more feeling
right side which gives you imagination and intuition.
